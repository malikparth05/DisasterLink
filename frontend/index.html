Mission Impact Strategy

The Problem

Fragmented humanitarian coordination and data silos lead to life-critical delays in resource allocation during regional disasters.

The Resolution

DisasterLink implements a 3NF Normalised Schema and ACID-Compliant Transactions with automated triggers, views, and stored procedures for zero-downtime relief operations.
